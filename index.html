About Us

Built on Craftsmanship & Trust

We're not just plumbers — we're problem solvers who take pride in every job.

Reliant Plumbing was founded with a simple mission: to deliver premium plumbing services with the precision of an engineering firm and the care of a family business. We serve homeowners, property managers, and businesses across the region with a commitment to transparency, speed, and lasting quality.

Every project — from a small faucet repair to a full commercial repipe — is treated with the same level of attention and craftsmanship. We use advanced diagnostic tools, premium materials, and a meticulous process to ensure every job is done right the first time.

Our team is fully licensed, insured, and continuously trained on the latest plumbing technologies. We believe in clear communication, fixed pricing, and standing behind our work with a 5-year warranty.
